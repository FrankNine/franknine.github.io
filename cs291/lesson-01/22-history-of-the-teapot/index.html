I confirmed with Jim Blinn on August 11, 2015, that the teapot was squished because it looked nicer. Picture here of Blinn with a 3D printed teapot, at SIGGRAPH 2015.

There are good articles about the history of the teapot by Frank Crow, S.J. Baker, and on Wikipedia. There are a number of iconic models and images in computer graphics. Some famous models can be found here and here; my own teapot code is available. Teapots still rule over all, with their own fan club and teapot sightings page. I photographed the whole collection. Oh, and Pixar made a short.

The demos shown can be run in your browser: teapot, teaspoon, and teacup.
